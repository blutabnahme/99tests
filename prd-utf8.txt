The platform addresses a growing gap in GermanyΓÇÖs healthcare system: patients increasingly lack convenient access to blood collection services, driven by declining family doctor practices, expanding telemedicine, and the specialized nature of functional and integrative medicine diagnostics.</p><p>The platform serves as an intermediary, ensuring legal compliance, quality assurance, and logistical coordination. Revenue is generated from both B2B service fees (charged to healthcare companies for organizing blood collection) and transactional commissions (retained from patient payments to blood collectors).</p><h2>Problem Statement</h2><ul><li>Declining family doctor practices reduce local blood-draw availability, especially for home visits.</li><li>Telemedicine growth widens the physical gap between therapist and patient, creating diagnostic bottlenecks.</li><li>Local laboratories typically require samples to be analyzed in-house, limiting test options.</li><li>Supraregional specialized laboratories (functional/integrative medicine) have no mechanism to organize nationwide blood sampling.</li><li>No centralized, legally compliant platform exists to match patients with qualified phlebotomists across Germany.</li></ul><h2>Solution Overview<strong> [NEW v3] </strong></h2><p>A web-based marketplace (with REST API for programmatic integration) that enables healthcare companies to upload patient cases, automatically matches them with nearby qualified blood collectors, supports flexible BC selection (HC-curated, patient-driven, or auto-assigned), facilitates appointment booking, manages payment processing (including dynamic travel fees), and ensures legal and quality standards are met throughout the process.</p><h1>User Roles and Personas</h1><p>The platform operates with four distinct user types, each with specific permissions, workflows, and interfaces.
